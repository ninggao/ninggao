    I am a Data Scientist at Microsoft Knowledge Graph team. Before joining Microsoft, I was a PhD student at the School of Information Studies of the University of Maryland, College Park. My advisor is Prof. Douglas W. Oard. My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Base Population, and Information Extraction.
